Phase I Findings

All laboratory error sources ruled out. Instrument calibration verified valid. Calculation checked by second analyst (Kiran Babu) and confirmed accurate. Triplicate results are reproducible and consistent. No assignable lab error identified.

Conclusion: ESCALATE TO PHASE II

OOS result is reproducible and not attributable to laboratory error. Full investigation required.

2

Phase II — Full Investigation

IN_PROGRESS
Assigned: qc.manager

Working Hypothesis

Moisture uptake occurred during transit or storage due to a packaging breach or inadequate humidity control in the supplier's warehouse. The API received under GRN-2026-042 may have been exposed to elevated humidity (>60% RH) prior to delivery.

Retest Protocol

Resample from 3 additional containers from the same GRN (containers 3, 7, and 12 of 20). Test in duplicate using Karl Fischer titration on INST-0003 and confirm on INST-0007. Compare with T=0 moisture from vendor CoA (reported: 1.8%).

Investigation Actions

DONE Request vendor CoA and transport log from Cipla Ltd (Vendor BU: Cipla-API-HYD)
IN_PROGRESS Retest moisture on 3 additional containers per retest protocol above
PENDING Inspect secondary packaging integrity of remaining containers from GRN-2026-042
