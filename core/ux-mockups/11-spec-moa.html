Procedure Details
Principle

Reversed-phase HPLC with UV detection at 243 nm. Separation on C18 column (150 × 4.6 mm, 5 µm). Mobile phase: 0.1% phosphoric acid (pH 3.0) / acetonitrile gradient. External standard quantification against reference standard.

System Suitability Criteria
  • RSD ≤ 2.0% for 5 reference injections
  • Tailing factor 0.8 – 2.0 for principal peak
  • Plate count NLT 2000 for reference peak
Reagents & Standards
  • · Paracetamol RS (Ph.Eur. CRS, assigned potency on CoA)
  • · Acetonitrile HPLC grade
  • · Orthophosphoric acid, 85%
  • · Water for chromatography
Calculation Formula
Assay (%) =
(Asample / Astd) × (Wstd / Wsample)
× Pstd × D × 100
